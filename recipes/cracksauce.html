Crack Sauce

Description

Try this amazing umami-packed Asian sauce. It’s a sweet and spicy sauce made from a combination of fish sauce, sugar, lime juice, shallots, garlic, cilantro, and chili peppers.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 small shallot, finely minced
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1-2 Thai bird’s eye chilis (or to taste), finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp water (optional, to thin out the sauce)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves completely
  2. Add the minced shallot, garlic, and chilis to the mixture. Stir to combine
  3. Add cilantro and mix everything together.
  4. Taste and adjust with more lime juice, sugar, or fish sauce as desired.
  5. If you prefer a thinner sauce, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.
