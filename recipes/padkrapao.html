Pad Krapao

Description

Pad Krapao is a Thai stir-fry dish with minced meat, garlic, chilies, and holy basil, seasoned with soy and fish sauces. Served over rice, often with a fried egg, it's a spicy, savory street food favorite.

Ingredients

  • 300g (10 oz) minced chicken, pork, or beef
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3-4 Thai chillies, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh holy basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 200 g jasmine rice
  • 1-2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients:

    • mince the garlic and chilies. set aside
    • wash and dry the basil leaves.

  2. Make the sauce

    • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, dark soy sauce (if using), and sugar. Stir until combined.

  3. Cook the stir-fry

    • Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and chilies.Stir fry until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
    • Add the minced meat and cook until fully browned, breaking it up with a spatula, 3-4 minutes.

  4. Season the dish:

    • Pour the prepared sauce over the meat. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the meat absorbs the sauce.
    • Toss in the holy basil leaves and stir for about 30 seconds until wilted. Remove from heat.

  5. Serve:

    • Spoon the stir-fry over steamed jasmine rice.
    • top with a crispy fried egg for an authentic touch
